What is IEEE

IEEE is the world's largest technical professional organization — 400,000+ members across 160 countries. It sets the standards that run the internet, power grids, and wireless networks. Not a club. Infrastructure.

// 02

Our Student Branch

IEEE SB LBSITW at LBS Institute of Technology for Women, Thiruvananthapuram. Active since 2015. We run technical workshops, paper presentations, and industry connects — year-round, not just during fests.

// 03

Why Ignite

Most tech fests are spectator sports. Ignite isn't. Every event demands hands-on work — code you write, pitches you deliver, problems you solve. The name isn't metaphor. It's instruction.

Common queries,
direct answers.

Any student currently enrolled in an undergraduate or postgraduate engineering or technology program. You don't need to be an IEEE member to participate — though membership does have its privileges during registration.

Participation certificates are issued to all registered attendees. Performance certificates (with rank) are issued to top three finishers in each competitive event. Workshop completion certificates require full attendance throughout the session.

Tech Quiz and Problem-Solving Challenge are team events with 2–4 members per team. Innovation Pitch allows teams of 1–3 members. Coding Challenge and workshops are individual. Resume Building and LinkedIn Workshop are individual sessions.

General registration is ₹150 per participant, covering up to three events. IEEE members get a reduced rate of ₹100. Payment is collected at the venue on the day of the event. Online pre-registration is mandatory for all participants.

Yes. Ignite is open to students from all institutions. Bring a valid college ID card. Outstation participants should note that accommodation is not provided by the organizing team — plan accordingly.

Bring your college ID, registration confirmation (printed or on your phone), and a laptop for the Web Dev Workshop and AI/ML Session. For the Coding Challenge, lab systems will be provided. A pen and notepad is recommended for Career Guidance and LinkedIn sessions.
